ABOUT US

Gallery



White Walls is born from the joint effort of 3 young entrepreneurs who decided to create a space that hosts a hand-picked selection of furniture pieces, lighting and home products.

With a love for adventure and new discoveries the three owners embarked in a journey to assemble an eclectic range of products from both upcoming and internationally recognized European designers.

White Walls Gallery showcases a diverse range of fine, bold and innovative work to cater to a young and trendy clientele. White Walls is also defined by unusual and singular designer pieces often combining functionality with affordability.

Located in a trendy Mar Mikhail occupied by rising designers, White Walls also functions as a showcase space for upcoming Lebanese artists and designers looking to exhibit their furniture and products.
